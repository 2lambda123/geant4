Shower profiles

Geometry

The geometry is a simple cylinder of homogeneous material. The cylinder is replicated longitudinaly (slice) and radialy (ring), in radiation length unit.
The pictures show a cylinder of 20 slices of 1 radiation length, and of 20 rings of 0.25 radiation length.
The incident particle is an electron of 5 GeV. Only the tracks of charged particles are drawn.
