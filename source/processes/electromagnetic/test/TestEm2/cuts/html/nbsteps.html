Stepping from Energy loss

Description

An electron of 1 GeV is travelling in an infinite medium (Aluminium)
Only energy loss and multiple scattering contribute : no delta ray, no brems.
The pictures (G4) (G3) shows 20 events overloaded.

The plots (1000 events) show :

  • the track length of the electron
  • the number of steps along the track
  • the distribution of the step size
